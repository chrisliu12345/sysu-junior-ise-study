$$
f'(x) = 3t^2-2\\
f''(x) = 6t\\
t=(0+3)/2=1.5\\
t_1=t-\frac{f'(t)}{f''(t)}=0.828968\\
t_2=t-\frac{f'(t_1)}{f''(t_1)}=0.816590\\
